oBSR Insights
  • Highest Monthly Conversion: June (0.6838) - This could be related to summer vacation planning.
  • Lowest Monthly Conversion: July (0.3384) - This might be due to browsing behavior without immediate booking intent.
  • Highest Day Conversion: Tuesday (0.5858) - Mid-week appears to be when users are most decisive.
  • Lowest Day Conversion: Wednesday (0.4438) - This shows a significant drop from Tuesday's peak.
Business Recommendations
  • Optimize search results and UI during high-conversion periods (June, Tuesday) to capitalize on user decisiveness.
  • Implement targeted interventions during low-conversion periods (July, Wednesday) such as limited-time offers.
  • Analyze the user experience factors that contribute to higher conversion on Tuesdays.
  • Consider timing marketing campaigns to align with seasonal conversion patterns.
  • Set conversion rate targets based on historical monthly and daily patterns.
